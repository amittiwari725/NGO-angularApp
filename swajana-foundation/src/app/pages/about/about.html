About SWAJANA Foundation

Founded with a vision to transform rural education in India, SWAJANA Foundation has been empowering children and communities for over 8 years.

Our Story

SWAJANA Foundation was born from a simple yet powerful belief: every child, regardless of their socio-economic background, deserves access to quality education.

Founded in 2016 by a group of passionate educators and social workers, our journey began when we witnessed the stark educational disparities in rural India. We saw bright, eager children whose potential was limited not by their capabilities, but by their circumstances.

What started as a small initiative to provide basic literacy to 50 children in a remote village in Rajasthan has now grown into a comprehensive educational movement reaching over 5,200 students across 150+ villages in multiple states.

Our name "SWAJANA" comes from Sanskrit, meaning "one's own people" or "community." This reflects our core philosophy of working with communities as partners, not just beneficiaries, in the journey toward educational transformation.
